Freedom

Licensed under the GPLv2, Linux's software license protects both the creators and users. You are at liberty to use your computer to your advantage and whatever is feasible. Code modification of anything in a Linux distribution is allowed and encouraged.

Power

Take control of your computer with a Linux distribution. Make it work for you with an unlimited set of options. Automate your entire system with the powerful built-in shell. Choose which updates you want and when you want them, no forced updates -- ever.

Privacy

Experience peace of mind as no mega-corporation spies on your desktop usage by default. Enjoy easy file system encryption to secure your data from thieves. With community audited source code, Linux distros are some of the safest operating systems in existance.
